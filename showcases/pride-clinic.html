Kenya Airways Medical Clinic HMIS

FortiCare is Kenya Airways’ in-house Hospital Management Information System (HMIS), built to support medical service delivery for staff, passengers, and walk-in clients. The platform manages patient registration, clinical documentation, medical history, pharmacy operations, stock management, and billing processes. Phase II focuses on enhancing the system by addressing functional gaps, improving usability, and aligning workflows with real-world clinical operations to deliver a more efficient and reliable healthcare system.

Project Overview & Goals

The goal of Phase II is to transform FortiCare into a fully integrated and efficient HMIS by closing functional gaps, redesigning workflows, and improving system usability. The project introduces optimized end-to-end patient care processes, enhances data integrity, and enables seamless interaction across clinical, pharmacy, billing, and administrative modules. It also supports better decision-making through improved reporting and structured data management.

Final Design & Solution

  • End-to-End Care Enablement: Fully integrated patient journey from registration to discharge.
  • Workflow Optimization: Streamlined clinical, pharmacy, and billing processes.
  • Improved Usability: Simplified interfaces for faster and more accurate data entry.
  • System Integration: Enhanced module interaction and reduced manual workarounds.
  • Data Integrity: Structured data capture and cleanup for accurate reporting and billing.

Impact & Results

  • Patient flow: Reduced processing time from ~15–20 mins to under 10 mins per visit.
  • Data accuracy: Cut duplicate entries and common errors by ~30–40%.
  • Staff efficiency: Fewer workarounds — most tasks now completed in one system instead of 2–3.
  • Billing: Noticeably fewer discrepancies due to cleaner, structured data.
  • User experience: Simpler flows = less back-and-forth, faster task completion.

Key Features

  • Patient registration and visit management.
  • Clinical documentation and medical history tracking.
  • Pharmacy and stock management modules.
  • Billing and payment processing.
  • End-to-end patient workflow support.
  • Reporting and analytics dashboards.
  • System integrations and module interoperability.
