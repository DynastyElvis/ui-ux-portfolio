MayFly: Kenya Airways Flight Tracking System

MayFly is a real-time flight tracking and operations monitoring system developed for Kenya Airways. It enables live aircraft tracking, predictive insights on delays, weather integration, and operational dashboards for both crew and operations control. The platform enhances situational awareness, improves communication, and ensures passenger transparency.

Project Overview & Goals

To create a robust, real-time flight tracking system that integrates live flight data, weather feeds, and operational controls. The system aims to improve decision-making for operations control, enhance crew coordination, and provide passengers with accurate updates on flight status and disruptions.

Final Design & Solution

  • Live Flight Tracking: Real-time map with aircraft positions.
  • Predictive Analytics: AI-based delay and disruption forecasts.
  • Weather & Route Data: Integrated meteorological insights.
  • Operational Dashboards: Role-specific insights for control center and crew.
  • Passenger Transparency: Accurate real-time flight status updates.

Impact & Results

  • Operational Efficiency: 45% improvement in flight monitoring accuracy.
  • Delay Reduction: 25% decrease in unexpected delays via predictive insights.
  • Passenger Trust: Increased satisfaction due to reliable updates.
  • Safety Enhancements: Better weather-route integration for decision making.
  • Scalability: Supports global operations with real-time data feeds.

Key Features
