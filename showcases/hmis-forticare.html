Adili Website: Ethical Resource Hub

The Adili Website provides access to Policies, Principles, Case Studies, Ambassador profiles and the Adili chatbot for the KQ Group.

Project Overview & Goals

To create a central website for KQ Group's ethical resources, including policies, principles, case studies, ambassador profiles, and the Adili chatbot.
