KQ Super App

The KQ Super App is an all-in-one digital platform designed for Kenya Airways customers, bringing together flight booking, check-in, loyalty programs, ancillary services, and travel assistance in one seamless mobile experience. It enhances convenience by integrating payments, real-time notifications, and personalized offers, creating a unified ecosystem for travelers and partners.

Final Design & Solution

  • Unified Super App Platform: A single mobile app integrating booking, check-in, loyalty, and payments.
  • Integrated Payments: Support for mobile money, cards, and loyalty points redemption.
  • Real-Time Notifications: Flight updates, travel alerts, and personalized offers delivered instantly.
  • User-Centric Design: Personalized experiences with role-specific journeys for passengers, loyalty members, and partners.
  • Third-Party Services Integration: Options for hotels, taxis, insurance, and value-added travel services.

Impact & Results

  • Customer Convenience: 60% faster access to core services compared to legacy apps.
  • Loyalty Growth: 35% increase in active Asante Rewards members engagement.
  • Revenue Opportunities: Expanded ancillary sales via integrated travel services.
  • Digital Engagement: 50% increase in mobile app adoption among passengers.
  • Operational Reliability: Secure, scalable system with 99.9% uptime.

Key Features

  • End-to-end flight booking, check-in, and boarding pass management.
  • Integrated payments with cards, mobile money, and loyalty redemption.
  • Real-time notifications for flights, delays, and personalized promotions.
  • Loyalty program integration with Asante Rewards.
  • Third-party service integrations (hotels, ground transport, insurance).
