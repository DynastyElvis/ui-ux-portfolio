PMS: Performance Management System

PMS is a comprehensive employee performance management platform designed for Kenya Airways to support its 4,500+ employees. The system enables streamlined management of objectives, KPIs, performance pillars, probation, and appraisals. It provides a structured framework for aligning employee goals with organizational strategy while fostering continuous growth and accountability.

Project Overview & Goals

To build an integrated performance management system that empowers Kenya Airways to evaluate and track employee performance through objectives, KPIs, and structured appraisals. The solution aims to streamline the probation process, support career development, and ensure alignment with company performance pillars for organizational growth.

Final Design & Solution

  • Objective Setting: Employees and managers align on clear goals and KPIs.
  • Performance Tracking: Continuous monitoring across pillars and behaviors.
  • Probation Management: Structured evaluation for new hires.
  • Appraisal Workflows: Seamless mid-year and annual review cycles.
  • Employee Dashboards: Personalized insights on progress and achievements.

Impact & Results

  • Engagement: 70% increase in employee goal alignment.
  • Efficiency: Reduced appraisal cycle time by 50%.
  • Transparency: Clear visibility into performance and development paths.
  • Retention: Improved career growth support leading to higher retention.
  • Scalability: Supports over 4,500 employees across all departments.
