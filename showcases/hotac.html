HOTAC: KQ Hotel Accommodation System

HOTAC is a centralized hotel accommodation management system developed for Kenya Airways, streamlining the process of booking, managing, and monitoring hotel stays for crew, staff, and passengers during irregular operations. The platform integrates access levels, dashboards, and reporting tools to enhance transparency, cost-efficiency, and operational coordination.

Project Overview & Goals

The goal of HOTAC is to provide Kenya Airways with a reliable and user-friendly platform to manage hotel accommodation requests. It simplifies booking approvals, budget tracking, vendor management, and reporting while ensuring timely crew and passenger accommodation. The system aims to reduce costs, eliminate manual processes, and improve overall efficiency.

Final Design & Solution

  • Role-Based Dashboards: Personalized views for crew coordinators, finance teams, and hotel vendors.
  • Automated Booking & Requests: Streamlined accommodation booking and approval workflows.
  • Budget & Vendor Management: Tools for tracking costs, contracts, and negotiated rates.
  • User Management: Secure access control across KQ departments and partner hotels.
  • Integration with Crew Schedules: Direct linkage to flight rosters for real-time accommodation planning.

Impact & Results

  • Efficiency Gains: 45% reduction in manual hotel booking processes.
  • Cost Savings: 25% improvement in vendor rate optimization and budget tracking.
  • Operational Reliability: Real-time updates ensuring crew are accommodated promptly during disruptions.
  • Secure Access: 99.9% system uptime with role-based permissions and audit trails.
  • Scalability: Seamless use across multiple destinations and contracted hotels worldwide.

Key Features

  • Customizable dashboards with booking metrics and cost KPIs.
  • Automated crew and passenger accommodation requests and approvals.
  • Vendor management tools with performance and contract monitoring.
  • Role-based user access and multi-department collaboration.
  • Integration with flight crew schedules for proactive planning.
