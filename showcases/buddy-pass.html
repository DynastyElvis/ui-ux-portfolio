Kenya Airways Buddy Pass Automation System

The newly launched Kenya Airways Buddy Pass System is a digital platform designed to streamline the annual staff buddy nomination program. It replaces a previously manual, email-driven process with a seamless, automated workflow covering buddy application, consent, HR validation, and ERP activation. The system enhances efficiency, reduces errors, and delivers a faster, transparent experience for staff, buddies, and HR teams.

Project Overview & Goals

The goal of this project was to digitize the end-to-end Buddy Pass process by eliminating manual paperwork and introducing a centralized system. The solution enables staff to nominate buddies, allows buddies to apply and complete payments, and empowers HR teams to validate and approve applications efficiently. The system integrates with iPride (ERP), iSafari, and Mpesa, ensuring real-time processing and improved operational efficiency.

Impact & Results (Anticipated)

The Buddy Pass Automation System significantly improves operational efficiency and user experience by eliminating manual processes and enabling near real-time processing.

  • Efficiency: Reduced HR workload and manual handling.
  • Accuracy: Minimized data entry errors.
  • Speed: Reduced activation time from days to near real-time.
  • Transparency: Improved visibility across all stakeholders.
  • User Experience: Simplified and intuitive journey for all users.

Key Features

  • Buddy nomination and application portal.
  • Consent and document submission workflow.
  • HR validation, approval, and rejection system.
  • ERP (iPride) and iSafari integration.
  • Mpesa payment integration.
  • Automated notifications (SMS, email, portal).
  • Real-time application tracking.
