Laza App: Your Ultimate Fashion Marketplace

The Laza app is a comprehensive e-commerce platform designed for clothware, offering a seamless shopping experience with integrated payment systems like Visa and M-Pesa. It features personalized recommendations, real-time order tracking, a virtual fitting room, and exclusive deals, making fashion retail accessible and enjoyable.

Project Overview & Goals

To develop a user-friendly mobile app that revolutionizes clothware shopping. The goal is to provide a platform with diverse payment options, advanced features like virtual try-ons, and personalized shopping experiences, driving customer engagement and sales.

Impact & Results

The Laza app revolutionizes online fashion retail with AI-driven personalization, seamless checkout, and virtual fitting, leading to higher sales and retention.

  • Increased Customer Engagement: AI-powered recommendations boosted user interaction by 30%.
  • Higher Conversion Rates: Optimized checkout flow reduced cart abandonment by 22%.
  • Enhanced User Experience: Virtual fitting led to a 40% reduction in size-related returns.
  • Stronger Brand Loyalty: Personalized deals increased repeat purchases by 18%.
  • Expanded Market Reach: Multi-currency payment integration drove a 25% rise in international orders.
