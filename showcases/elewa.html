Clean Squad App: Revolutionizing Home Service Marketplaces

Clean Squad is an all-in-one home service marketplace designed to connect skilled professionals with clients in need of top-quality services. Whether you're a technician, handyman, or service provider, Clean Squad makes it easy to find jobs, submit proposals, and communicate with clients seamlessly.

Project Overview & Goals

To create a user-friendly mobile app that bridges the gap between service providers and clients, simplifying job discovery, communication, and service delivery. The goal was to design an intuitive platform that enhances efficiency and trust for both parties.
