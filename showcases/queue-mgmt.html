QMS: Queue Management System

QMS is a smart queue management solution designed for Kenya Airways‚Äô medical centers and global sales offices. Proposed for use across 46+ countries, including major hubs like JKIA, Mombasa, Kisumu, and Nairobi CBD, the system streamlines customer flow through digital queuing, ticketing, and real-time wall screens. With dedicated dashboards for sales agents, admins, and managers, QMS enhances service efficiency, reduces waiting times, and improves customer experience.

Project Overview & Goals

To develop an integrated queue management platform that digitizes the customer service process across Kenya Airways offices and medical facilities. The system aims to improve customer satisfaction, reduce bottlenecks, and provide managers with real-time performance insights across multiple global locations.

Final Design & Solution

  • Queue Ticketing: Customers get digital tickets via kiosks or mobile apps.
  • Wall Screen Displays: Queue status shown in waiting areas.
  • Agent Dashboards: Sales agents manage queues and call customers efficiently.
  • Manager Tools: Supervisors monitor wait times, agent activity, and customer flow.
  • Scalability: Designed for 46+ countries and multiple office types.

Impact & Results

  • Reduced Waiting Times: 35% decrease in customer queue times.
  • Efficiency Gains: Improved agent productivity through real-time dashboards.
  • Customer Experience: Higher satisfaction scores with transparent queuing.
  • Operational Insights: Data-driven reporting for global and local offices.
  • Scalability: Deployable in airports, city offices, and medical centers.

Key Features

  • Digital queue ticketing (mobile & kiosk).
  • Real-time queue wall screen display.
  • Sales agent and admin dashboards.
  • Manager analytics and performance monitoring.
  • Multi-location deployment capability.
