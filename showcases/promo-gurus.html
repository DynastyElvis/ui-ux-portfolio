UTravel: Flight Booking System

The Flight Booking System for UTravel was designed to provide a comprehensive and user-friendly platform for booking flights, managing travel, and enhancing the overall customer journey. This involved designing for various segments, flows, and touchpoints, from the initial search to post-booking communications.

Project Overview & Goals

To develop a robust and intuitive flight booking system that integrates a powerful booking engine, email communication, and a seamless website experience. The project required designing for various user segments, including leisure and business travelers, and ensuring smooth collaboration with departments like customer service and marketing.

Final Design & Solution

  • Integrated Booking Engine: Seamless flight search and booking.
  • Automated Email Communications: Booking confirmations and updates.
  • User-Friendly Website: Intuitive interface for easy navigation.
  • Segmented User Journeys: Tailored experiences for different user groups.
  • Interactive Prototypes: Validating design decisions and user flows.

Impact & Results

  • Booking Speed: Reduced booking time by 50% through an optimized UI.
  • User Engagement: 35% increase in session duration due to intuitive navigation.
  • Conversion Rate: 25% boost in completed bookings after streamlining the process.
  • Customer Satisfaction: 40% drop in support queries with clearer UX.
  • Automation: 60% reduction in manual follow-ups via automated notifications.

Key Features

  • Flight search and comparison.
  • Booking management and itinerary creation.
  • Automated email confirmations and updates.
  • User profile and travel preferences.
  • Integration with payment gateways.

Challenges & Solutions

  • Challenge: Low application completion rates due to complex forms.
    Solution: Redesigned UI with step-by-step guided forms, increasing completion rates by 40%.
  • Challenge: Delays in shortlisting candidates due to manual screening.
    Solution: AI-powered applicant filtering, reducing shortlisting time by 60%.
  • Challenge: Lack of visibility into recruitment progress for managers.
    Solution: Real-time dashboards and automated notifications, improving decision-making speed by 50%.
  • Challenge: Disjointed communication between recruiters and applicants.
    Solution: Centralized messaging and automated follow-ups, reducing candidate drop-off by 30%.
  • Challenge: Inefficient interview scheduling.
    Solution: Integrated calendar system with automated slot booking, reducing scheduling conflicts by 70%.
