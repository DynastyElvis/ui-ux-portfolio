Upcycling Studio: Sustainable Creations from the Skies

The Upcycling Studio is an innovative e-commerce platform by the Fahari Innovation Hub, Kenya Airways Group, showcasing and selling unique upcycled products made from aircraft materials. This platform promotes sustainability, features diverse product ranges, and includes essential e-commerce sections like cart, sign-in, about, and FAQs.

Project Overview & Goals

To create a user-friendly e-commerce platform that drives sustainability by selling upcycled products from aircraft materials. The goal is to provide a seamless shopping experience with integrated payment systems like Visa and M-Pesa, while promoting environmental consciousness and generating revenue for the Kenya Airways Group.
